Dog Catcher

  • High Concept: Play as a dog who has mysteriously forgotten his powerful abillities and must cope with suddenly remembering them.
  • Player Experience Goals: Surprise, tension and possibly some humor.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: Player must figure out the best way to finish a platforming level while staying safe from enimies and apapting to sudden changes in their characters controls. Controls are listed at the top of the screen and randomly change every few seconds.
  • Prototype goal: Rapid and unpredictable mapipulation of controls and mechanics during gameplay.
  • Features: Unqiue set of controls and elements of randomness.
